Simple facts

-There are few deaths a year in Las Matas.

-Rafael Pimentel, celebrated story teller, and Bergson Rosario, laureate poet and pedagogue, both known around the country, were born in Las Matas De Santa Cruz.

-Pedro Antonio Pimentel, heroe of The Restauration of The Republic, is said to have been born in this municiple.

-Francis Drake visited the area where las Matas De Santa Cruz is located, before departing to Rioacha, Colombia, to sack that city.
