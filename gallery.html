Where is it?

Las Matas is a small town, compared to the big cities in the country, but as a municiple section, is quite big.

People in Las Matas De Santa Cruz live in small houses, which are more often than not build by their owners. Each house brings to life each owner's feelings.

Also, the landscape is beautiful, as we can see on the pictures here. Bananas are one of the main crops of this land, and I wanted to show that in some of the pictures here.
